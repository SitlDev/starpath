Common Inquiries

The Bibliophile’s Guide

What is the condition of the books?
We source high-quality pre-loved books. While they may have minor creases or the "scent of history," we ensure no structural damage, heavy markings, or missing pages.
Can I specify my preferred genres?
Yes. All members gain access to the Collector’s Vault, where you can "Express Interest" in specific genres or titles to guide our curation process.
How does shipping work?
Standard shipping is included in all memberships. We ship in the first week of every month. Each box is tracked and insured for its historical value.
