Curator's Tasting Note

For this month's selection, we recommend pairing the atmospheric prose of Morgenstern with a 70% Single-Origin Dark Chocolate. Let the richness of the cocoa melt as you encounter the first circus tent. The bitterness of the chocolate grounds the ethereal magic of the story, creating a perfect sensory grounding.

Enjoy your monthly sanctuary.
