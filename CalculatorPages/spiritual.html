What is a 'Master Number' in numerology?
In numerology, the numbers 11, 22, and 33 are considered Master Numbers. Unlike other numbers, they are not reduced to a single digit during the calculation process. These numbers are thought to carry a higher vibrational frequency and represent a greater responsibility for spiritual growth and leadership. Our tool correctly identifies these numbers to ensure your profile is accurately modeled.
How does the Moon Phase affect me?
While the scientific community focuses on the moon's physical effect on tides and biological rhythms, many spiritual traditions believe the lunar phases—from the "New Moon" of intentions to the "Full Moon" of manifestation—reflect our internal emotional cycles. Tracking your birth moon phase or the current cycle can provide a symbolic context for your current energy levels and focus.
Is Numerology a predictive science?
Numerology is best viewed as a system of archetypes rather than a predictive science. It provides a mirror for self-reflection by identifying recurring patterns and traits. Like a psychological framework, it offers a way to categorize and understand our innate tendencies, allowing for more intentional personal development rather than "predicting" a fixed future.
