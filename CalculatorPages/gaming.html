What is the 'Soft Pity' system in Gacha games?
Most modern games (like Genshin Impact or HSR) don't use flat rates. Instead, after a certain number of unsuccessful pulls (e.g., 75), your odds of a rare drop increase exponentially with each subsequent pull until you reach "Hard Pity" at 90. Our calculator models this curve so you can budget your currency effectively.
How do I optimize my character's DPS?
The key to maximizing damage is balancing Crit Rate and Crit Damage. The industry standard "1:2 ratio" (e.g., 60% Crit Rate to 120% Crit Damage) is mathematically the most efficient way to scale output. Our DPS tool accounts for base damage and attack speed to show you exactly how each stat shift impacts your average output.
How much storage does a 1080p60 stream take?
Streaming at 1080p60 with a 6,000kbps bitrate (6 Mbps) generates approximately 2.7 GB of data per hour. If you are recording a high-quality master copy at 50 Mbps, that requirement jumps to nearly 22 GB per hour. Planning your disk space with our storage estimator prevents recording failures during critical gaming sessions.
